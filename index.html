About

Hi, I'm Lynn.

A 22-year-old UX/UI & Visual designer from Amsterdam. I love combining user experience and design to create a complete digital experience. Currently in my final year of Communication and Multimedia Design at the Hogeschool van Amsterdam.

I've always loved being creative. Since I was little, I've enjoyed crafting and drawing — that's why I feel right at home at CMD. I focus mainly on Graphic Design and UX/UI, because it lets me express my creativity and design products for different users with different needs. And sometimes, I also enjoy getting my hands dirty with a bit of front-end development.
